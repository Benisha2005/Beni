About Us

Country Explorer is a simple web project designed to help users explore detailed information about countries. This includes:

  • National flags
  • Population statistics
  • Capital cities
  • Official languages
  • Famous landmarks and tourist places
  • Currency details
  • Weather updates of selected countries

Our goal is to make learning about countries easy, interactive, and fun for students and curious minds alike. The project provides a user-friendly interface where all information is accessible on a single page.
